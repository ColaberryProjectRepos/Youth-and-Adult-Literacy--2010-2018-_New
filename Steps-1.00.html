Problem Statement 1

Literacy, the ability to read and write, is fundamental for personal and professional growth in today's world. Shockingly, over 30% of the population in Sub-Saharan African countries still lacks these essential skills. The overall literacy rate for women in Africa is 65%, compared to 77% for men. Unfortunately, this issue is not limited to this region alone. The literacy rate for adults in Asia was 78.3% in 2020, up from 70.6% in 2010. Addressing low literacy rates is crucial to create a more equitable and inclusive society where everyone has access to the tools they need to succeed. It's time to take action and work towards a brighter future for all by ensuring that basic literacy skills are accessible to everyone.
